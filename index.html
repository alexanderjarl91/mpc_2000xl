AKAI MPC2000XL

A javascript tribute to the legendary sampler

Back in the days, hip-hop music was created by chopping up bits and pieces of old records and rearranging them to make a beat. Some of the greatest producers of that era used an Akai MPC to chop sounds and arranging them into a sequence.

MPC2000XL USER MANUAL

----------------------



1. You either use your mouse to click the pads or use the assigned keys on your keyboard.


2. You can click the play button (or hit spacebar) for a backing track.


3. You can click the stop button (or hit spacebar again) to stop the backing track.




This application was created to play around with my daughter and show her the basic mechanics of a drum machine and sampler.
